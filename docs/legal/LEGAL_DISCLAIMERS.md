**Our AI technology has inherent limitations:**

- **False Positives:** AI may identify errors that don't actually exist

## Professional Consultation Recommendations

### When to Consult an Attorney
**Seek legal counsel when:**
- 🔴 **Significant Financial Impact:** Potential damages exceed $1,000
- 🔴 **Foreclosure Risk:** Immediate threat to property ownership
- 🔴 **Complex Legal Issues:** Multiple potential RESPA violations
- 🔴 **Servicer Non-Response:** No response after 30-60 days
- 🔴 **Bankruptcy Considerations:** Financial distress affecting mortgage
- 🔴 **Class Action Potential:** Widespread servicer misconduct

### When to Consult Financial Professionals
**Seek financial advice for:**
- 💰 **Mortgage Modification:** Loan term or payment changes
- 💰 **Refinancing Decisions:** Market timing and qualification
- 💰 **Investment Strategies:** Property investment considerations
- 💰 **Tax Implications:** Deductions and financial planning
- 💰 **Retirement Planning:** Mortgage payoff strategies

### When to Contact Housing Counselors
**HUD-approved counselors help with:**
- 🏠 **Foreclosure Prevention:** Pre-foreclosure assistance
- 🏠 **Workout Options:** Alternative payment arrangements
- 🏠 **Loss Mitigation:** Avoiding foreclosure through servicer programs
- 🏠 **Budgeting:** Financial management and planning
