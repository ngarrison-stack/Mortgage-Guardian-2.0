# Follow-up Nurture Campaign for MI Companies

## Campaign Structure Overview

### Campaign Objectives
1. **Maintain Top-of-Mind Awareness** during long sales cycles
2. **Build Trust and Authority** through valuable content
3. **Educate on ROI** with progressive value demonstration
4. **Create Urgency** around competitive advantage
5. **Drive Meeting Requests** at optimal timing

### Target Personas
- **C-Suite**: CEO, COO, CRO (ROI + Strategic focus)
- **Innovation Leaders**: SVP Innovation, CTO (Technology focus)
- **Risk Management**: VP Risk, Analytics Directors (Data focus)
- **Operations**: VP Operations, Claims Directors (Process focus)

## Multi-Channel Delivery Strategy

### Email Marketing
**Frequency**: Weekly for active prospects, bi-weekly for long-term nurture
**Personalization**: Company-specific data and messaging
**Automation**: Drip campaigns based on engagement level

### LinkedIn Engagement
**Strategy**: Social selling and thought leadership
**Content**: Native LinkedIn posts and articles
**Frequency**: 3-4 posts per week

**LinkedIn Content Calendar**:
- Monday: Industry insights
- Wednesday: Case studies and success stories
- Friday: Thought leadership and trends

### Content Marketing
**Blog Publishing**: 2 articles per month
**Guest Posts**: Industry publications
**Webinars**: Quarterly educational sessions
**Whitepapers**: Monthly deep-dive reports

### Direct Mail
**Trigger Events**: After 3 months of email engagement
**Materials**: Custom ROI reports, case study booklets
**Frequency**: Quarterly for high-value prospects

### Event Marketing
**Conference Follow-up**: Immediate post-event nurture
**Webinar Attendance**: Automated follow-up sequence
**Workshop Invitations**: Exclusive roundtable events

## Engagement Scoring and Triggers

### Lead Scoring Model
**Email Engagement** (0-25 points):
- Open: 1 point
- Click: 3 points
- Reply: 5 points
- Forward: 5 points

**Content Engagement** (0-50 points):
- Download: 10 points
- Webinar attendance: 15 points
- Demo request: 25 points
- ROI calculator use: 20 points

**Website Behavior** (0-25 points):
- Page visit: 1 point
- Multiple pages: 3 points
- Return visit: 5 points
- Time on site >5 min: 5 points

### Trigger Actions
**Score 25-49** (Warm):
- Increase email frequency
- Personal LinkedIn connection
- Custom content creation

**Score 50-74** (Hot):
- Direct sales outreach
- Executive briefing offer
- Pilot proposal delivery

**Score 75+** (Very Hot):
- C-suite engagement
- Custom ROI model
- Immediate meeting request

## Success Measurement and Optimization

### A/B Testing Framework
**Subject Lines**: Test 2 versions for each campaign
**Content Format**: Long-form vs. short, video vs. text
**Send Times**: Test optimal days and times
**Call-to-Action**: Test different CTA buttons and text

### Monthly Review Process
1. **Performance Analysis**: Open rates, clicks, conversions
2. **Content Optimization**: Top and bottom performing pieces
3. **Persona Refinement**: Adjust messaging based on engagement
4. **Campaign Adjustments**: Modify frequency and timing

### Quarterly Business Review
1. **Pipeline Impact**: Qualified leads generated
2. **Revenue Attribution**: Deals influenced by nurture
3. **Campaign ROI**: Cost per qualified lead
4. **Strategy Refinement**: Major campaign adjustments

This comprehensive nurture campaign ensures consistent engagement with MI company prospects throughout the extended sales cycle, building trust and demonstrating value while maintaining top-of-mind awareness for when decision timing aligns.
