## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Rules
- Include specific numbers/ROI when possible
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use company name for personalization
- A/B test different approaches

### Content Guidelines
- First sentence: Hook with specific benefit
- Second paragraph: Problem + solution
- Third paragraph: Proof/credibility
- Final paragraph: Clear call to action
- Always include meeting scheduling link

### Timing Optimization
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Best times: 10-11 AM, 2-3 PM EST
- Avoid: Mondays, Fridays, end of month
- Follow up on different days/times
