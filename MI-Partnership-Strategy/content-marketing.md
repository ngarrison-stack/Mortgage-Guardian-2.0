# Content Marketing Strategy for MI Company Authority Building

### Primary Goals
1. **Establish Thought Leadership** in AI-powered risk management
2. **Build Trust and Credibility** with MI decision makers
3. **Generate Qualified Leads** through valuable content
4. **Educate Market** on servicing error prevention
5. **Drive Inbound Interest** from target companies

### Target Audience Segments
- **C-Suite Executives**: Strategic decision makers
- **Risk Management Leaders**: Technical stakeholders
- **Innovation Teams**: Technology evaluators
- **Industry Analysts**: Influence multipliers

## Platform-Specific Strategy

### LinkedIn (Primary Platform)
**Objectives**: Thought leadership, direct executive engagement
**Frequency**: 5 posts per week
**Content Mix**: 40% original, 30% curated industry, 30% company news

**Content Formats**:
- **Native Posts**: Industry insights, quick tips
- **Articles**: Detailed analysis and thought leadership
- **Videos**: Product demos, expert interviews
- **Documents**: Reports, whitepapers, case studies

**Engagement Strategy**:
- Comment on target executives' posts
- Share relevant industry news with insights
- Participate in industry group discussions
- Direct message value-driven content

### Company Blog
**Objectives**: SEO, lead generation, detailed education
**Frequency**: 4 posts per month
**Average Length**: 1,500-2,500 words

**Content Categories**:
- **How-To Guides**: Implementation strategies
- **Industry Analysis**: Market trends and insights
- **Technology Explanations**: AI and machine learning
- **Case Studies**: Detailed success stories

**SEO Keywords**:
- "mortgage insurance claims prevention"
- "AI mortgage servicing errors"
- "mortgage default prevention"
- "servicing error detection"
- "MI company ROI"

### Industry Publications
**Target Publications**:
- **Inside Mortgage Finance**: Monthly contributed article

- **HousingWire**: Quarterly thought leadership piece
- **Mortgage Professional America**: Expert interviews

**Contributed Content Strategy**:
- Industry trend analysis

### Email Newsletter
**Frequency**: Bi-weekly
**Audience**: 2,500+ industry professionals
**Content Mix**: 60% original insights, 40% curated industry news

**Newsletter Sections**:

**Quarterly Themes**:
