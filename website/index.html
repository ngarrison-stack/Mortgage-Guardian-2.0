How It Works

Three steps. Zero guesswork.

1

Upload Your Statements

Take a photo or upload PDFs of your mortgage statements. Our OCR engine — powered by AWS Textract — extracts every data point, even from the worst scans.

2

AI Runs the Audit

Our forensic engine cross-references every payment, fee, and balance against federal regulations (RESPA, TILA, ECOA, FDCPA) and state law. Every violation flagged with the exact statute, section, and calculated damages.

3

Get Your Weapons

Mortgage Guardian generates legally-formatted dispute documents — Qualified Written Requests, Notices of Error, CFPB complaints, demand letters — ready to send. Your servicer has 30 days to respond by law.

The Regulatory Vacuum

The CFPB has been gutted — 90% staff cuts, enforcement frozen, supervision halted. State AGs are scrambling to fill the gap. Industry experts are calling it a "wild west" of consumer protection.

But the laws are still on the books. RESPA, TILA, ECOA, FDCPA all have private rights of action. Homeowners can still enforce them — they just need the tools. That's Guardian AI's entire thesis.
