# Privacy Policy for Mortgage Guardian 2.0

**Effective Date:** October 4, 2025
**Last Updated:** October 4, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Mortgage Guardian 2.0 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- Name and contact information
- Device identifiers and mobile device information
- App usage data and analytics

### 2.2 Financial Information
- **Bank Account Data**: Through our integration with Plaid, we access your bank account information, transaction history, and account balances solely for mortgage audit purposes
- **Mortgage Documents**: Documents you upload for analysis, including loan statements, payment records, and correspondence
- **Transaction Data**: Payment history and transaction patterns related to your mortgage

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Information
- Device type, operating system, and app version
- Usage patterns and feature interactions
- Error logs and performance data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Mortgage Audit Services**: Analyze your mortgage loan servicing for errors and RESPA violations
- **Document Analysis**: Process uploaded documents using AI to identify discrepancies
- **Bank Data Verification**: Cross-reference bank transactions with mortgage payment records
- **Service Improvement**: Enhance app functionality and user experience
- **Legal Compliance**: Meet regulatory requirements and respond to legal requests

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Third-Party Service Providers
- **Plaid**: Secure bank account connectivity and transaction data retrieval
- **Anthropic (Claude AI)**: Document analysis and pattern recognition services
- **AWS**: Secure cloud infrastructure and data processing

### 4.2 We Do Not Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to authorized personnel only
- **Secure Infrastructure**: AWS-hosted with industry-standard security practices
- **Biometric Authentication**: Face ID/Touch ID for app access

### 5.2 Bank-Level Security
- Partnership with Plaid ensures bank-grade security for financial data
- No storage of bank login credentials
- Read-only access to specified accounts

## 6. Data Retention

- **Financial Data**: Retained only as long as necessary for audit analysis, typically 30-90 days
- **Documents**: Stored securely until analysis is complete and user chooses to delete
- **Usage Data**: Aggregated analytics data may be retained for service improvement
- **Account Data**: Deleted within 30 days of account closure

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access and Control
- View and download your data through the app
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Revoke bank account access at any time
- Delete uploaded documents

### 7.2 Communication Preferences
- Opt out of non-essential communications
- Control push notification settings

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.
